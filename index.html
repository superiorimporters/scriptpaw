The Gap

Human health solved this.
Animal health hasn't.

Doceree, OptimizeRx, and Populus built a billion-dollar ecosystem connecting pharma brands to physicians inside EHRs and telehealth platforms. The veterinary world has no equivalent — despite billions in pharma marketing spend with nowhere precise to go.

01

Animal pharma brands have no digital POC channel

Zoetis, Elanco, Merck Animal Health spend billions on brand marketing — but have no way to reach veterinarians at the exact moment of prescribing, or pet owners during a vet visit.

02

Vet platforms are unmonetized digital real estate

Vet PIMS and telehealth platforms sit inside the clinical workflow all day. They have no mechanism to serve contextually relevant pharma messaging — leaving revenue on the table.

03

The infrastructure is mature. The network doesn't exist yet.

The supply side — Covetrus, ezyVet, Vetster, Dutch — is ready and actively seeking monetization partnerships. The demand side is spending. Nobody has connected them.
